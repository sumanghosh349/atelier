Who we are

Atelier imbibes an entirely new concept of serving handloom designing with solutions that are latest, attractive, and driven by a team who thrive to excel in their services in providing a high return on investment.

Atelier is the brainchild of Amrita Ghosh,who laid the foundation of this company with her vision to see a difference in designing industry.

Atelier officially started up in 2015.The startup has been journeying through trial and errors and with each passing day it has seen so many avenues and successfully passed all challenges it has faced.

What we do

Handloom is not just about wearing a certain fabric, it is connected to Indian history and politics deeply. Supporting prints,texture made on handloom was a trend common only among the arty, hipster crowd.

We engage in manufacturing an exclusive range of ladies and gents designer wears,home linens,craft items & accessories. Ladies-Kurti,T-Shirts,Sarees,Kurta,Punjabi,Table cloth,Craft items.
